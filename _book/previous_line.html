Chapter 8 Previous line

Previous line identifier is [pl]

8.1 Definition

Instrument previous line refers to any anticancer treatment received, either for a prior cancer or for the current cancer, before the current line.

The current line is the most recently administered one that contains immune checkpoint inhibitors. It can be ongoing at the time of index cardiotoxicity, or may have been interrupted.
